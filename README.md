1. EPITHALAMIVM:
OR,
A Nuptiall SONG, with a Narrative
of Loves progresse: Consecrated to the honoured fame
of His much valued Friends, Mr. ABRAHAM CULLEN,
and Mrs. ABIGAIL RUSHOUT, the blest Objects of
this Triumphant Solemnity: To whom I wish
a Life enduring continuance of this
Dayes felicity,
Whilst I remain obliged to the Honour of Their
especiall favours, and rest their most
humble Servant
LEONARD LAURENCE.
